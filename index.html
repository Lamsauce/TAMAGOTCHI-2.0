As time goes by, your pet will get hungry, tired, and bored. The lower the bar gets, the more attention your pet needs! Click on the 'Feed', 'Sleep', and 'Play' buttons to keep your pet happy!

Your pet won't survive if you don't take care of it! If your pet's needs are yellow, it needs some attention. If your pet's needs are red, it's in danger!

GAMEPLAY
This version of Tamagotchi is based on the original, official Tamagotchi. The official version includes: age and weight of your pet.
The pet's needs are different (hunger, happiness, and discipline) than this version (hunger, energy, and entertainment).
The official pets can also leave droppings the owner has to clean up! Luckily, we don't pick up pet droppings here!
